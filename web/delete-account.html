Delete your account & data

You can permanently delete your Tempo account and the data associated with it at any time. This page explains how, what gets removed, and how to request deletion if you can no longer access the app.

Delete your account in the app

The fastest way is directly inside Tempo:

  1. Open the Tempo app and make sure you're signed in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and associated personal data are permanently removed.

Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app (for example, you've uninstalled it), email us from the address linked to your account and we'll process the deletion for you:

jacobalexanderroberts2011@gmail.com  — subject line "Delete my Tempo account."

We'll verify your request and complete the deletion, typically within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the personal data tied to it, including:

  • Your account and profile (email, name, goals, and preferences).
  • Your training plan, scheduled and completed workouts, and session feedback.
  • Your body measurements and weight history.
  • Any progress photos you uploaded.
  • Your registered notification/device tokens.

Deleting specific data instead

If you'd rather keep your account but remove particular information, you can delete individual measurements, progress photos, and logged workouts within the app, or email us to request removal of specific data.

Retention note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We may retain a limited amount of information where required to comply with legal obligations, after which it is deleted. For more detail, see our Privacy Policy.
