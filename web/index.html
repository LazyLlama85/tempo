How it works

Set it up once. It runs your week.

Three steps, then Tempo handles the planning so you don’t have to think about it.

01

Connect your calendar

Link Google or Apple Calendar in seconds. Tempo only sees when you’re free or busy — never what the events are.

02

Tempo learns your week

Tell it your goal, experience, and equipment. Tempo finds the real gaps in your schedule and books workouts like meetings.

03

It adapts when life changes

A meeting runs long or a day blows up? Tempo reshuffles the plan automatically — you keep moving forward instead of falling behind.
