Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Tempo ("Tempo," "we," "us") builds your training schedule around your real life. This policy explains what information the Tempo mobile app collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights you have. We aim to collect only what we need to make the app work for you.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and, where available, your name. You can also use the app as a guest without an account.
  • Fitness & profile data. Goals, training experience, availability (work, school, sleep, and off-days), scheduled and completed workouts, and the feedback you give on sessions.
  • Body measurements. Bodyweight and measurement history you choose to log.
  • Progress photos (optional). If you attach photos to your measurements, they are stored privately to your account. You can delete them at any time.

Information accessed on your device

  • Calendar. With your permission, Tempo reads your calendar to schedule workouts around your commitments. Calendar events are used on your device to plan sessions and are not sold.
  • Notifications. With your permission, Tempo sends pre-workout reminders and retention nudges. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Information collected automatically (only if enabled)

  • Analytics. If product analytics are enabled in a given build, we collect anonymized usage events (for example, screens viewed and features used) to understand and improve the app.
  • Crash & diagnostic data. If crash reporting is enabled in a given build, we collect crash logs and basic device/diagnostic information to fix problems.

2. How we use your information

  • Generate and adapt your personalized training plan.
  • Schedule workouts around your availability and reschedule when you miss or struggle with sessions.
  • Track your progress, measurements, and trends over time.
  • Send workout reminders and relevant notifications.
  • Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the app.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. How your information is shared

We share information only with service providers that help us run Tempo, and only as needed:

ProviderPurpose
SupabaseSecure cloud database and authentication that stores your account and app data.
Apple / GoogleSign-in (authentication) when you choose those options.
Expo Push / APNs / FCMDelivery of the notifications you opt into.
Analytics & crash-reporting providers (if enabled)Aggregate usage insights and crash diagnostics to improve the app.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and our service.

4. Data security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and stored with our cloud provider under access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app. When you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data, except where we are required to retain it to comply with legal obligations.

6. Your rights & choices

  • Delete your account and data. In the app, go to Profile → Delete Account. This removes your account and associated personal data. You may also email us to request deletion.
  • Access & correction. You can view and update much of your data directly in the app, or contact us for help.
  • Permissions. You can revoke calendar, notification, and photo permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise them.

7. Children's privacy

Tempo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
